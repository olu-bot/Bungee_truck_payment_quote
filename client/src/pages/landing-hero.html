What's Inside

Every Feature Exists Because a Carrier Needed It

Nothing was built for a demo. Every feature here solves a real problem that came up on the job — and gets used daily.

AI-Powered Route Chat

Just type "Toronto to Hamilton to London" and costs show up. It handles typos, conversational input, and multi-stop routes — no menus, no dropdowns.

Multi-Stop Route Builder

Add as many stops as you need. Distances calculate automatically, the map updates live, and every leg gets priced separately — deadhead from your yard included.

Equipment Cost Profile Wizard

Dry Van, Reefer, Flatbed — set up your truck's real numbers in about 5 minutes. Payments, insurance, maintenance, fuel consumption, driver pay. It all lives in one profile.

3-Tier Pricing Advice

See 20%, 30%, and 40% margin quotes with quality labels — Low, Fair, Good, Great, or Outstanding. It helps you decide in seconds if a load is worth taking.

Live EIA Fuel Prices

Fuel data pulls automatically from the EIA — no manual updates. When fuel jumped 25% over a weekend in early March, carriers on Bungee had accurate quotes Monday morning. Everyone else was still using last week's numbers.

Cross-Border Ready (CA/US)

CAD ↔ USD, metric ↔ imperial, L/100km fuel consumption — all handled. Built by a Canadian carrier doing cross-border runs, so it works the way you actually need it to.

IFTA Fuel Tax Estimator

See per-jurisdiction fuel tax breakdown for every route. Know your tax liability across state and provincial lines before you accept the load — no spreadsheet required.

Analytics Dashboard

Track win rate, revenue, top lanes, and margins across your entire quote history. See what's working and where you're leaving money on the table.

AI Pricing Suggestions

Get data-driven price recommendations based on your own win/loss history. The app learns your lanes and tells you the optimal margin to quote at.

Team Management

Owner, Admin, and Member roles with role-based access. Control who can edit profiles, manage team, or just build quotes — everyone has exactly what they need.

Mobile-First Design

Quote from anywhere — in the cab, at the dock, on the road. Bottom tab bar, compact layout, and one-tap quoting via chat. Built for the phone in your pocket.
