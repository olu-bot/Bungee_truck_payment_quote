Built for Carriers

Everything You Need to Quote Like a Pro

Built by carriers, for carriers. These features are ready to use right now — no credit card, no trial, no catch.

1

AI-Powered Route Chat

Type "Toronto to Hamilton to London" and the route auto-builds with costs calculated. Handles spelling mistakes and conversational input.

2

Multi-Stop Route Builder

Add unlimited waypoints, auto-calculate distances via OSRM, and see your route on an interactive map. Drag to reorder stops.

3

Cost Profile Wizard

Set up Dry Van, Reefer, or Flatbed profiles covering truck costs, insurance, fuel, wages, and dock/detention rates.

4

3-Tier Pricing Advice

Instantly see quotes at 20%, 30%, and 40% margin with quality labels: Low, Fair, Good, Great, or Outstanding.

5

Live EIA Fuel Prices

Fuel costs update automatically from US EIA data. Always quote with today's real fuel price, not last month's guess.

6

Cross-Border & Multi-Currency

Seamlessly quote Canada-US routes with automatic CAD/USD conversion, local/long-distance detection, and deadhead from your yard.

7

Quote History & Tracking

Save every quote with customer notes, mark won/lost, and review full route details. Never lose a quote again.

8

Team Management

Admin vs. Sales roles with secure access. Sales sees pricing advice only — no internal cost data exposed to your team.
