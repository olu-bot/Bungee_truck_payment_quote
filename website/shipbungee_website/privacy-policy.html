Privacy Policy
Effective Date: March 27, 2026

Bungee Supply Chain Ltd. (“Bungee,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use Bungee Connect (the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Company name and fleet size
  • Operating country, state or province, and city
  • Preferred currency and unit of measurement (metric or imperial)

1.2 Cost Profile Data

When you create a Cost Profile, you provide financial data about your trucking operation, including monthly fixed costs (truck payments, insurance, maintenance, permits), working schedule, driver compensation rates, and fuel consumption. This data is used solely to generate quotes for your routes.

Your Cost Profile data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.2+ in transit. We do not have access to your decrypted Cost Profile values. Our team, including administrators, cannot view, read, or analyze your individual cost data. The encryption keys are managed through Google Cloud’s Key Management Service (KMS) and are not accessible to Bungee employees.

1.3 Route and Quote Data

When you build routes and generate quotes, we store origin and destination addresses, distances, drive times, calculated costs, margin percentages, and any notes you add. This data is associated with your account for your quote history.

We also derive anonymized lane-level metadata from route and quote activity — such as corridor (e.g., origin region to destination region), equipment type, distance band, and rate range — which is stripped of any identifying information and contributed to our Aggregated Data pool. This derived data cannot be traced back to your account, company, or Cost Profile.

1.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect limited technical data to maintain and improve the Service:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and features used within the Service
  • Timestamps of interactions
  • Error logs and performance metrics

We do not use third-party analytics trackers or advertising pixels.

1.5 Fuel Price Data

We fetch publicly available diesel fuel prices from the U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA) and estimate Canadian regional prices. This data is cached locally in your browser and is not personally identifiable.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing the Service: generating routes, calculating costs, producing quotes, matching loads, and powering platform features based on your Cost Profile and preferences
  • Account management: authenticating your identity, managing your workspace, and communicating important service updates
  • Market Intelligence: creating Aggregated Data by combining anonymized, de-identified data points (such as lane corridors, equipment types, distance bands, and rate ranges) across all users to produce pricing indices, benchmark analytics, rate trend reports, and data feeds
  • Premium features: delivering intelligence back to you, such as lane-level rate comparisons, pricing recommendations, and market benchmarks derived from Aggregated Data
  • Data licensing: making Aggregated Data and Market Intelligence available to third parties (e.g., shippers, brokers, 3PLs, financial institutions) under commercial agreements. Licensed data is always anonymized and aggregated — it never identifies individual users, companies, or Cost Profiles
  • Service improvement: analyzing aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to identify bugs and improve features
  • Legal compliance: meeting regulatory obligations and responding to lawful requests from authorities

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information, individual Cost Profile data, or any data that identifies you or your company to any third party. Only Aggregated Data that has been fully de-identified and cannot reasonably be linked to any individual user may be used in Market Intelligence products or licensed commercially.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Infrastructure

Your data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted on Google Cloud Platform infrastructure. Data is stored in North American data centers with physical and logical access controls.

3.2 Encryption

We employ multiple layers of encryption to protect your data:

  • Data in transit: All communications between your browser and our servers use TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Data at rest: Cost Profile financial data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption via Google Cloud KMS
  • Authentication: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt and are never stored in plaintext

Zero-access architecture for Cost Profiles: Even Bungee administrators cannot decrypt or view your individual cost data. The encryption is designed so that only your authenticated session can access your Cost Profile values.

Aggregated Data is separate from Cost Profiles. When you generate a quote, anonymized metadata (lane corridor, equipment type, distance band, rate range) is derived and contributed to our Aggregated Data pool. This derived data is stored separately from your encrypted Cost Profile, contains no identifying information, and is used to power Market Intelligence features and products. The zero-access architecture applies to your individual Cost Profile values — not to the anonymized, aggregate-level statistics that are produced from collective platform activity.

3.3 Access Controls

Access to production systems is restricted to essential personnel, protected by multi-factor authentication, and logged for auditing. Team members you invite to your workspace can view shared Cost Profiles within your organization, but no one outside your workspace has access.

4. Data Sharing

4.1 Your Personal and Cost Profile Data

We share your personal information and Cost Profile data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Google Cloud Platform (hosting and storage), Google Maps Platform (routing), Firebase (authentication). These providers process data on our behalf under contractual obligations.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, we may disclose information to the extent necessary to comply.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the successor entity. We will notify you in advance.

We do not share your individual Cost Profile data, account information, or any personally identifiable data with other carriers, brokers, shippers, or any commercial third party.

4.2 Aggregated Data and Market Intelligence

We may share, license, or sell Aggregated Data and Market Intelligence products to third parties, including shippers, freight brokers, third-party logistics providers (3PLs), financial institutions, and data analytics firms. This data is:

  • Fully anonymized and de-identified — it cannot reasonably be used to identify any individual user, company, or Cost Profile
  • Derived from collective platform activity across thousands of users, not from any single user’s data
  • Presented at a statistical level (e.g., average rates on a corridor, regional pricing trends, equipment-type benchmarks) that makes re-identification of individuals practically impossible

We apply industry-standard de-identification techniques and will never release Aggregated Data where the sample size for any data point is too small to ensure anonymity. We require all third-party data licensees to agree not to attempt to re-identify individual users from Aggregated Data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Account information and Cost Profiles are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Quote history is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backup copies are purged within 90 days of account deletion
  • Aggregated Data that has already been de-identified and incorporated into Market Intelligence products is retained, as it is no longer attributable to you and cannot be meaningfully “deleted” from a statistical aggregate

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data for certain purposes

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at inbox@shipbungee.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Compliance with Canadian Privacy Law

Bungee Supply Chain Ltd. complies with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy legislation. We collect, use, and disclose personal information only with your knowledge and consent, for identified purposes, and in a manner consistent with Canadian law.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on your consent (provided at account registration) and our legitimate interest in providing the Service. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

8. Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal browser storage:

  • Authentication tokens: Essential cookies to keep you signed in (session-based, not persistent)
  • Theme preference: Stored in localStorage so your dark/light mode choice persists
  • Fuel price cache: Cached in localStorage for up to 24 hours to reduce load times

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, or analytics pixels.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Service is intended for business use by individuals aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we learn that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.
