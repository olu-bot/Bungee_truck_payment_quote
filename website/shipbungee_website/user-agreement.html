1. Definitions

“Service” means the Bungee Connect web application, including route building, cost profiling, quote generation, fuel pricing, market intelligence, load matching, and all related tools, features, and platform capabilities as they evolve over time.

“User” or “you” means any individual or entity that creates an account or accesses the Service.

“Cost Profile” means the encrypted financial data you enter about your trucking operation, including vehicle costs, driver pay rates, insurance, fuel consumption, and related operating metrics.

“Quote” means a route-based cost estimate generated by the Service using your Cost Profile and real-time data such as fuel prices and distances.

“Workspace” means your company’s shared environment within the Service, accessible to authorized team members.

“Aggregated Data” means data that has been de-identified, anonymized, and combined across multiple users so that it cannot reasonably be used to identify any individual user, company, or Cost Profile. Examples include average lane pricing by corridor, regional rate trends, equipment-type benchmarks, and market indices.

“Market Intelligence” means analytics products, pricing indices, benchmark reports, lane-level rate insights, and data feeds derived exclusively from Aggregated Data.
